Paul McConnell

Overall Synopsis of Paul's 43 years in the Development & Construction Industry

Paul McConnell is currently acting in an advisory capacity as CEO for a Saudi real estate/developer based in Riyadh, prior to this he was a C-Suite Executive COO for Saudi PIF responsible for overseeing large scale portfolio developments. With over four decades of experience, Paul has developed strong financial expertise in Feasibility, Master Planning, Development, Design, Construction and Fast Track Delivery of high-profile iconic International Resorts.

Paul has spent twenty-six years of his career working in the Middle East on some of the largest and most iconic projects in the world at CEO, C-Suite COO, Senior Director and General Manager level. He has worked very closely with high-profile personalities including Mr Donald Trump, The Al Thani family, The Maktoum family and various government entities worldwide such as FIFA.

Throughout his career, Paul has worked as a Developer, a Consultant in Project Management and as a Main International Contractor, delivering some of the most prestigious fast-track projects in the world. He held the position of Senior Director on the iconic Katara Towers (5 and 6 Star International Hotels) representing the flagship for the FIFA World Cup 2022 in Qatar, and served as Senior Director for the main developer of the iconic International Resort Palm Island Dubai, overseeing developments of the main iconic towers international resorts, being responsible for projects exceeding $2 Billion USD in value.

Paul has managed and delivered a diverse portfolio including Iconic High-Rise Towers, Major Retail Developments, 5 and 6 Star International Hotels, Mixed Use High-End Residential Developments, Gated Communities, International Airports, Grade A Offices, Hospitals, Schools, Stadiums, Equestrian facilities and 4 Major International Golf Courses. All projects include associated infrastructure development.

Paul has worked for high-profile companies at senior level including PIF, Katara Hospitality, Emaar, Nakheel, Al Futtaim, Mace, and Arabtec Construction.
